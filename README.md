🚀 **Excited to Share My Latest Project: AI-Powered Resume Screening & Job Matching Platform**

🔍 **The Problem:**
Recruiters often spend a huge amount of time manually reviewing resumes, and even then, biases or overlooked details can affect decision-making. At the same time, candidates may miss out on opportunities simply because their resumes are not optimized for screening systems.

💡 **My Solution:**
I developed an intelligent system that automates resume analysis and matches candidates with relevant job roles based on their skills, experience, and qualifications.

⚙️ **Key Features:**

* 📄 **Resume Parsing:** Extracts important information like skills, education, and experience from resumes
* 🧠 **Skill-Based Matching:** Compares candidate profiles with job descriptions to find the best fit
* 🤖 **Machine Learning Integration:** Uses ML techniques to improve matching accuracy
* 📊 **Data-Driven Insights:** Helps in better candidate evaluation and shortlisting

🛠️ **Tech Stack:**

* Python
* Machine Learning & NLP
* Scikit-learn, Pandas, NumPy
* frontend: React.js
